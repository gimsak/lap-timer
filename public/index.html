How to Change Camera

To change your default camera for websites like this lap timer, follow these steps for your browser:

Chrome:
  1. Open Chrome Settings (⋮ menu → Settings)
  2. Click "Privacy and security" in the left sidebar
  3. Click "Site Settings"
  4. Scroll down and click "Camera"
  5. Under "Default behavior", select your preferred camera from the dropdown
Safari:
  1. Open System Settings/Preferences
  2. Click on "Camera"
  3. Select your preferred camera as the default
  4. Note: Safari uses your system's default camera settings
Firefox:
  1. Open Firefox Settings (☰ menu → Settings)
  2. Click "Privacy & Security" in the left sidebar
  3. Scroll to "Permissions" section
  4. Click "Settings..." next to Camera
  5. Select your default camera from the dropdown at the top
After changing your default camera, refresh this page and click "Reset" to use the new camera.
